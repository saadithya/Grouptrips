Powerful Features

Everything you need to find and plan your perfect group trip

🗺️

Interactive Map View

Explore trips visually on an interactive map. Tap on markers to see trip details instantly and navigate destinations effortlessly.

🔍

Advanced Filtering

Filter by budget range, location, trip type (Domestic/International), travel dates, and providers to find exactly what you're looking for.

❤️

Favorites & Saved Trips

Save your favorite trips for quick access later. Build your personal travel wishlist and compare options easily.

🏢

Multiple Providers

Access trips from various trusted travel providers in one place. Compare offerings and find the best deals.

💰

Real-time Pricing

View up-to-date pricing in your local currency. Budget filters help you find trips within your price range.

📅

Date-based Search

Find trips that match your available travel dates. Plan ahead with clear start and end date information.

📍

Detailed Trip Information

View comprehensive trip details including destination, inclusions, descriptions, and direct booking links.

🌐

Domestic & International

Browse both domestic and international trips with clear categorization and destination details including country, state, and city.

📸

Rich Media Gallery

Browse trip photos to get a visual preview of your destination and accommodations before booking.

App Permissions

We only request permissions necessary to provide you with the best experience

📍 Location Access

Used to show your current position on the map and provide location-based trip recommendations.

💾 Storage Access

Required to save your favorite trips locally on your device for offline access.

🌐 Internet Access

Needed to fetch trip data from our servers, load images, and access booking links.

📷 Camera (Optional)

May be used for future features like scanning QR codes for trip bookings or uploading travel photos.
